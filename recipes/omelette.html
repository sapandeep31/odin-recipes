Simple Egg Omelette

Description

Enjoy a quick and delicious simple egg omelette for breakfast or brunch. This recipe is easy to make and customizable with your favorite ingredients. It's a classic breakfast dish that's ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
  • Optional fillings (e.g., grated cheese, diced bell peppers, onions, ham, spinach)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk, and season with salt and pepper. Whisk the mixture until well combined.
  2. Step 2: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil. Make sure it coats the bottom of the pan.
  3. Step 3: Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook without stirring for a minute or two until the edges start to set.
  4. Step 4: Add any desired fillings to one half of the omelette. Fold the other half over the fillings to create a half-moon shape.
  5. Step 5: Continue cooking for another minute or until the omelette is set but still slightly runny on top.
  6. Step 6: Carefully flip the omelette using a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until it's fully cooked and slightly golden brown.
  7. Step 7: Slide the omelette onto a plate, garnish with additional toppings if desired, and serve hot.
