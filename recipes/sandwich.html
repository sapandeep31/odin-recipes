Mayo Sandwich

Description

Indulge in a delicious mayo sandwich for a quick and satisfying meal. This recipe is easy to make and perfect for a snack or light lunch. Creamy mayonnaise, fresh veggies, and your choice of fillings make for a tasty and customizable sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced bell peppers (optional)
  • Sliced onions (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Lay out four slices of bread on a clean surface or plate.
  2. Step 2: Spread a tablespoon of mayonnaise on each slice of bread.
  3. Step 3: Place a lettuce leaf on two of the slices.
  4. Step 4: Add sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, and any optional fillings on top of the lettuce.
  5. Step 5: Season with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Step 6: Top each sandwich with the remaining slices of bread, mayonnaise side down, to create two sandwiches.
  7. Step 7: Gently press the sandwiches together and cut them in half if desired.
  8. Step 8: Serve your mayo sandwiches as a delightful snack or a light meal.
